Vibing Account Deletion

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This page explains how to request account deletion and what data is deleted or retained.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Request Deletion

  1. In-app: Profile -> Settings -> Account -> Delete Account.
  2. Email: Send from your registered email to support@vibing.now with subject: Account Deletion Request.

Typical processing time is within 72 hours, and we may verify account ownership before deletion.

Data Deleted

  • Profile information (alias, bio, interest content).
  • User-created vibes and room participation metadata tied to your account.
  • Signals and related interaction data associated with your account identifier.

Data That May Be Retained

  • Records required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or security.
  • Limited operational logs and backups for a short retention period.

Retained records are minimized and kept only as long as required for the purposes above.
