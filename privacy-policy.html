Privacy Policy for Vibing

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Vibing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and retain information when you use the Vibing mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email, vibing name (alias), and account preferences.
  • Profile information: avatar seed, bio, interest tags, and optional age bracket.
  • Activity data: vibes created/joined, room participation, and in-app interactions.
  • Signals data: sent/received signals and mutual connection states.
  • Location data: approximate location for nearby discovery and crossed-path features.
  • Device and diagnostics: app/device metadata for reliability and security.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide matching, discovery, and temporary room experiences.
  • To generate your private daily recap experience.
  • To deliver essential notifications you opt into.
  • To detect abuse, moderate content, and enforce safety policies.
  • To debug, secure, and improve product performance.

3. How Information Is Shared

  • In-app visibility: other users may see your alias/profile elements according to your privacy settings.
  • Location protection: we do not expose exact GPS coordinates to other users.
  • Service providers: we use infrastructure vendors (for example, database/hosting providers) to operate the service.
  • No sale of personal data: we do not sell personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain account data while your account is active and for a limited period after closure when necessary for legal, anti-abuse, fraud prevention, or security obligations.

5. Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion in-app or by email. See the full deletion procedure and retention details on: Account Deletion Instructions.

6. Children's Privacy

Vibing is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material updates will be reflected with a revised "Last updated" date.
