Child Safety Standards

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Vibing ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to the safety of all users, and takes a zero-tolerance approach to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This page describes our standards and practices to prevent, detect, and respond to any content or behavior that endangers minors.

1. Age Requirement

Vibing is intended for users aged 17 and older. Users who are found to be under the minimum age are removed from the platform. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

2. Zero Tolerance for CSAE

We have a strict zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming behavior or solicitation of minors
  • Trafficking or exploitation of minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting CSAM in any form

Any user found to be engaging in such behavior will be immediately banned and reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Vibing provides multiple ways for users to report child safety concerns:

  • In-app reporting: Every vibe, message, and user profile includes a "Report" option accessible from the overflow menu. Users can report content or accounts they believe involve a minor in an unsafe situation.
  • Email: Reports can be sent directly to oscar@data-driven.tech.

All reports are reviewed promptly. Content flagged as potential CSAM is removed immediately pending review, and confirmed cases are reported to NCMEC via the CyberTipline.

4. Content Moderation

Vibing employs the following practices to keep the platform safe:

  • User-generated content (vibes, messages) is subject to community guidelines and our moderation policies.
  • Accounts that violate our child safety policies are permanently removed.
  • We review flagged content and take action within 24 hours for reports involving potential harm to minors.

5. Compliance

Vibing complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent laws in other jurisdictions. We report confirmed CSAM to the appropriate national authorities and cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.
